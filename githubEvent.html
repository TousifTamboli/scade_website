Teacher's Day Event

The SCADE Club, under the Department of CSE (Cyber Security & Data Science), successfully organized an event on the occasion of Teacher's Day to express the students' gratitude for the dedication and hard work of their teachers. The event served as a meaningful gesture to recognize and appreciate the efforts of the faculty in shaping the academic and professional journeys of the students.


The event featured a variety of fun and interactive games organized especially for the teachers, where they competed against one another with enthusiasm and a sense of camaraderie. Teachers displayed true team spirit, enjoying the lighthearted competition while fostering a deeper connection with their students outside the academic environment. The atmosphere was filled with laughter and energy, creating a memorable experience for both the faculty and students. The day concluded on a warm note, with students sharing heartfelt messages, expressing their gratitude for the dedication, mentorship, and invaluable impact the teachers have had on their academic and personal growth. As a token of appreciation, small mementos were presented to the teachers, symbolizing the students' respect and admiration. The session was handled by Prof. Swati Gajarlewar as the Activity Coordinator under the guidance of Dr. Deepika Ajalkar HOD CSE(CS & DS) and Dr. R D Kharadkar, Campus Director, GHRCEM.

The event, hosted by Tejashvi Tiwari and Abhay Sonkusare, also featured an engaging quiz competition. With around 80 students in attendance and coordination by Prof. Swati Gajarlewar Ma'am, the event was a resounding success, setting a strong foundation for SCADE's future initiatives
