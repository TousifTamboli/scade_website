Zero Day: Newbie Unveil - SCADE’s First Event at GHRCEM, Pune

The Zero Day: Newbie Unveil event marked a significant milestone for SCADE at GHRCEM, Pune, bringing together students and faculty for an inspiring day. The event commenced with a warm welcome for Masira Ma'am and Deepika Ajarkar Ma'am, who inaugurated the occasion. This was followed by a token of appreciation from Masira Ma'am to Deepika Ma'am, reflecting the department's strong camaraderie.

Deepika Ma'am delivered an insightful speech, emphasizing the importance of practical and technical knowledge to meet industry standards. She encouraged Cyber Security students to start practicing with tools like Kali Linux and announced new partnerships with industry leaders like Kali.io and Wisdom Sprouts, aimed at enhancing students' practical skills.


Mishra Sir then spoke about the significance of internships and original projects, stressing the need to avoid copyright infringement and the value of creating innovative, patent-worthy work and publishing research papers.

Next, Mr. Pranit Siddam, the new lead of SCADE GHRCEM, took his post with an inspiring and promising speech. He introduced the SCADE team and their work in various domains. Following this, Mr. Tousif Tamboli, the Tech Lead, showcased the official SCADE GHRCEM website. He guided the audience through the site, explaining its features and exploring it in detail. Tausif also highlighted the coding languages and text tags used to create and host the website, making the audience well-versed with its technical aspects

The event, hosted by Tejashvi Tiwari and Abhay Sonkusare, also featured an engaging quiz competition. With around 80 students in attendance and coordination by Prof. Swati Gajarlewar Ma'am, the event was a resounding success, setting a strong foundation for SCADE's future initiatives
